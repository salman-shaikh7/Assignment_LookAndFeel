About ExpenseTracker

A simple application to track and manage your expenses.

Project Overview

ExpenseTracker is a web application designed to help users manage and track their expenses efficiently. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can add, view, and analyze their spending habits. The project aims to provide users with an easy way to control their finances and make informed budgeting decisions.

Key features of the ExpenseTracker include:

  • Track and categorize expenses
  • View a history of all expenses
  • Get statistical insights into spending trends
